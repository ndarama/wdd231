Amahoro Stadium Ticket Portal

Site Name Explanation: The name “Amahoro Stadium Ticket Portal” directly connects the platform to the iconic Amahoro Stadium in Kigali, Rwanda, known for its rich sporting history. It highlights the purpose of the website—to provide a seamless ticket purchasing experience for events, including the upcoming match between Rwanda and Senegal.

Site Purpose

This website is designed to offer fans a user-friendly platform to purchase tickets for the upcoming football match between Rwanda and Senegal at Amahoro Stadium. The platform will provide detailed information about the event, ticket categories, pricing, and exclusive benefits to ensure that every fan finds the perfect matchday experience.

Ticket Categories

The ticket options for the match are divided into four categories to cater to different preferences and budgets:

  • VIP: Premium seating with exclusive access to hospitality lounges, best views, and complimentary refreshments.
  • Premium: Superior seating with enhanced comfort and better sightlines for an elevated match experience.
  • Regular: Standard seating providing a great view of the action along with essential amenities.
  • Economy: Affordable tickets offering access to the game with a basic yet enjoyable experience.

Scenarios

  • Scenario 1: How can I easily compare the four ticket categories for the Rwanda vs Senegal match to choose the one that best fits my needs?
  • Scenario 2: What exclusive benefits are offered to VIP ticket holders during the match at Amahoro Stadium?

Color Schema

The website employs a dynamic color scheme that reflects both the energy of football and the heritage of Rwanda:

  • Primary Color (#003366): Applied to headings, navigation elements, and key call-to-action areas for a professional look.
  • Secondary Color (#f9f9f9): Used as the background for sections to maintain a clean and modern layout.
  • Accent Color (#e31b23): Utilized on buttons and highlights to draw attention and evoke excitement.

Typography

The following font selections ensure both style and readability:

  • Montserrat: Used for headings and navigation to provide a bold, modern appearance.
  • Roboto: Applied to body text and paragraphs for optimal readability and a clean design.
